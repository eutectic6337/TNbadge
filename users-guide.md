# TNbadge User's Guide

## charge

- connect battery

- silde switch OFF / *ON*

- slide switch *enable epaper update* / blink LEDs

- connect USB-C cable to charge

## wear

- connect battery

- silde switch OFF / *ON*

- slide switch enable epaper update / *blink LEDs*

## modify

- silde switch OFF / *ON*

- slide switch *enable epaper update* / blink LEDs

- connect USB-C cable to computer
